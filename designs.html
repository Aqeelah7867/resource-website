This is a gallery where you can browse and find helpful links to get started!

Floral designs

Floral designs are a very beginner friendly as they mostly use repeated linework and some intricate stitches to create the flowers, these are reccomended to start with to get used to the flow of things then once you master these you can start to get more detailed and complex in your designs!

Object and Animal designs

For designs that are more lively and for embellishing a certain area on your fabric these are perfect for you!

Line Work

For wanted to write out a message or practice your lettering, these designs show how to mark up fabric and fill in the letters or words you would like to spell out!
